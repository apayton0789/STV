How to use this planner effectively

1. Review the clock & overview

You have until Thursday June 25, 5pm to get invoices submitted. The tool shows exactly how much is left for each program.

2. Add from suggestions or custom

Click any suggestion card to instantly add a realistic line item. Or use the quick-add form for your own ideas. Keep descriptions specific (who, what, when, how many people).

3. Update status as you move

Change status from "Planned" → "Invoice Requested" → "Invoiced". This helps leadership see progress at a glance when you export.

4. Export & share

Use the Export to Excel button. The file includes a summary + your planned items in a clean format that finance and leadership can read. You can also re-upload that same file later to continue planning.

5. Print for meetings

Click the print icon in your browser (or Ctrl/Cmd+P). The page is optimized with clean tables and the deadline reminder. Great for bringing to a quick sync with program staff or leadership.

Tip: If new invoices come in before you finish planning, click "Adjust Remaining" on the planner page to update the number. The tool will recalculate everything.
